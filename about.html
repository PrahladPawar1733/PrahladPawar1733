Vision We Have

Sleep is the platform on which nutrition, exercise and mindfulness reside.
But these pillars for your health aren’t nearly as efficient if your sleep’s not working.
Sleepify helps you to better health through better sleep. At its heart, it tracks your sleep cycles by listening to your sounds. It analyses those sounds by help of ever-evolving algorithms, and presents the results to you and helps you understand your sleep with unique data analysis and graphs.
